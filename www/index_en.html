The Berlin-Vegan App

With the "Berlin Vegan Guide", you've got quick access to an overview of the vegan food and shopping possibilities in Berlin, even while you're out and about.

  • over 170 restaurants, takeaways and icecream parlours
  • more than 90 cafés with soya milk available
  • 230 bakeries with a complete description of what's on offer
  • a further 100 shopping possibilities in the area of cosmetics, groceries and organic produce
download for Android download for iPhone

Quick, practical and local

We've divided the information up into frequently used categories. After you've selected a category (e.g. restaurants), the results will be sorted automatically and shown in order of distance from your current location. With the help of the number of stars, you can also see how vegan-friendly a place is.

Guide takes opening hours into consideration (very practical when you need fresh bread rolls on a Sunday morning!) and also allows you to activate your own filters.

Of course, the guide is not just suitable for vegetarians and vegans but also for all people who are trying to live their life in a greener and more animal friendly way.
